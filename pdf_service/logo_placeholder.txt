SAPPCON Logo Instructions

1. Add the SAPPCON logo as 'logo.png' in this directory (pdf_service/)
2. The PDF generator will automatically detect and use it

Recommended specifications:
- Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
- Size: 200x80 pixels (or maintain aspect ratio)
- High resolution (300 DPI) for crisp PDF rendering
- Logo should work well on white backgrounds

Current brand colors in use:
- Primary: #2968CE (SAPPCON Blue)
- Secondary: #B4975F (SAPPCON Gold)

If logo.png is not found, the generator will use "SAPPCON" text as fallback.
